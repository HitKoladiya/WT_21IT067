SPOURAL 2K23- THE MOST AWAITED EVENT !!

The most anticipated event for Charusatians, SPOURAL 2k23, will take place from January 23 to February 11, 2k23. The spoural is a celebration of the yearly cultural and sports week. Each year, the event is held with the goal of fostering students' overall growth and bringing to light any untapped talents among them. Aside from artistic competitions like paintings, elocution, debate, the Mr. and Miss Charusat Pageant, ad mad show, photography, documentary film making, quiz, clay modelling, and others, there are also performances of singing, dance, fashion shows, mimicry, drama, and more. Sports competitions like basketball, kabaddi football, table tennis, and many more are organised as off-stage activities. To take part in these fantastic activities, kindly register below. Together, let's make it a huge success with wonderful memories.
